Why this is the right job for the AI layer

  • Mechanized gates go green when a suppression is added — the suppression silences them. That blind spot is exactly what the audit covers.
  • Soft before, hard after. Pre-submit audit is advisory — approving a flagged suppression is the override, and you can run vrg-submit-pr on a "red" anytime. The Phase-5 post-PR audit is the one that blocks merge (TBD #2).
  • Detection vs judgment (future seam). Spotting a net-new suppression is mechanizable (a diff check) — could become a cheap pre-filter later; the judgment ("removable without hurting integrity?") and the approval stay human/AI.
  • Start simple: standards compliance + new-suppression escalation. Everything else is additive.
