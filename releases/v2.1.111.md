
# Release 2.1.111 (2026-07-04)

## Bug fixes

- **accept adhoc sentinel in issue-create/epic-move cross-org guard (#2124) (#2151)**
Phase 1 (epic #85). The cross-org guard in vrg-issue-create and vrg-epic-move skipped only the 'standing' sentinel; extend it to skip 'adhoc' too, since 'adhoc' (like the deprecated 'standing' alias) resolves within the repo's org (.github) and never diverges cross-org. Guard predicate becomes 'not in (standing, adhoc)'; docstrings/comments name adhoc as the current form. Without this, --epic adhoc was parsed as a literal ref and failed.
